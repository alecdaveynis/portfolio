Projects

Project 1 - VBTrain

VBTrain is an AI-powered volleyball training platform I developed to bring advanced feedback tools to players who don’t have consistent access to a coach. Built with Python, OpenCV, Streamlit, and OpenAI, it analyzes uploaded game film to track players, evaluate rotations, and deliver insights on positioning, errors, and performance statistics. What began as a tool to elevate my Cornell Club Volleyball team has evolved into a scalable platform with potential impact for athletes at all levels.

Project 2 - LiftHeavy

LiftHeavy is an app for tracking weightlifting progress and setting personal records. Users can log lifts (bench, squat, deadlift, overhead press), set custom goals, and visualize progress through dynamic progress bars, weekly improvement charts, and benchmark comparisons against the community.

Project 3 - Pricedle

Pricedle is a game I developed, inspired by Wordle, that challenges players to guess the price of a randomly selected item from an extensive database of Costco’s food products. With each guess, the game provides hints, guiding players on how far off they are until they land within 5% of the actual price. Test your skills and check out the project on my GitHub!
